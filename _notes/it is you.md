---
title: it is you
date: 2026-03-15
type: note
---
I’ve been thinking about how brief conversations can linger longer than they should. This is me sitting with one of those echoes.

---

> I hope it's you
though I don't know you yet
but
maybe one day I could say
is it you
while you could say to me
the same

> how can a
fleeting conversation
make me
flutter and falter
without a care
for consequence

> to call it just a moment
would be too small

> but to call it cathartic
makes it a lie 

> so even if I don't want this
I am glad
for the excuse
to press smooth
what is wrinkled
and leave the rest
